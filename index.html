FineApp

An app designed to educate users on finances and move them towards putting their financial savings to use with lenders!

In order to best target and conduct our developement we curated a survey to guage general understananding of finances

To further our grasp on the direction of our developement we cunduected interveiws to get verbtim quotes from interveiwees on what's important to our target demographic

After our surveys and interveiws we synthesized our data into a user persona

Interation and developement

After having gone through a research phase, we began creating components and working on mid to hi-fi wireframes of what our app might look like.

  • Social Circles
    One of the three USPs we decided to focus on was a social circle that would act as a hib for users to talk about ways that they used the app and develope their own strategies.
  • Info Hub
    The second of the third USPs is a mainstay feature found nearly everywhere: the home menu. For our app we wanted our home page to act as a way to track the progress of learning, savings, and social interactions
  • Getting Educated
    Our last and third USP is our educational integrations. Following developement and funding of the platfrom, we then went and found finance experts to condense down the feild of finance into bite sized snippets of knowledged for users to learn about and progressively re-educate themselves about the nuances of finance, or at very least just have the knowledge they need on hand, when they want it.
